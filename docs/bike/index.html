How to join

Currently the Carlmont Bike Club meets every week in E1 on Mondays. Attend a meeting if you are interesting in joining. Other ways to join include contacting any bike club member you know or any officer, and registering for the online bike club system. You do not need to attend Carlmont high school to bike with the club.

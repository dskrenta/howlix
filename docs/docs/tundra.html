The Tundra Biome Intro

Tundra is the coldest of all the biomes. Tundra comes froms the word tunturi meaning treeles plain. It is noted for its frost-molded landscapes, extremely cold tempetures, little precipitation, poor nutreint pool. The two major nutreints are nitrogen and phosphorus. Nitrogen is created by biological fixation, and phosporus is created by precipitation. Tundra is seperated into two types the Arctic Tundra, and the Alpine Tundra.

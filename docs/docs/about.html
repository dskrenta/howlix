The new way students search

I’m sure you, and your classmates make dozens of searches a day, which are related for school. But how often do you find all the info you need, immediately, with one click, given to you in easy to read and friendly format. Harvix is a start up search engine, created solely by kids with the vision to create a search engine, solely for kids. Harvix has 3 key features that heavily differentiate our self from our main competitors, Google, Bing, and other search engines.

Students, Searching, Harvix

How often do you find most of the information you need for your report, in one click, or on the iPhone? What about in a friendly social layout, we can tell you, you probably haven’t, until now. Harvix gives you customized search results, for your project, and your criteria. On other search engines, you will receive a list of links with titles and snippets of the site. On Harvix you get much more including Instant computed information on the search, notable facts on the topic, an article about the topic, our spam-free web results, and lots of information you could not find anywhere else.

Search + Facebook = Social Search!

When someone says search, and someone says social, you think of two, very different things. But, what if you mix the two? Now you can chat with your school friends about projects, ask them questions, and receive advice from other students. Harvix has done something, no other search engine has ever successfully done, mix relevant search results, and revolutionary social features. On Harvix you can ask your classmates questions, have a conversation with them, and send them helpful information. All of these help contribute to your ultimate research experience.

Your search, your eyes

Did you know that most of our competitors keep every search, every email, and anything you’ve ever written on their site? Here at Harvix, we feel this is unfair, that is why we collect no personal data. All we collect are your general analytics information, so we can learn more about our customers. We feel that your searches, your emails, your chats, and your projects are for your eyes only, not the nosy eyes of others. So, when you use Harvix, we guarantee a safe, search engine, that will not, and cannot, use any of your information against you.

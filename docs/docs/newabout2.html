About Harvix

Harvix is a research engine designed by students, for students.

Created by students, for students

Harvix's mission is to let students discover and share academic content in simple, and meaningful ways.

Instant Information

Harvix provides Instant Information such as a well formed article, facts about the query, data that is relevant to your search, and much more. Harvix’s Instant Information is so effective you’ll often ﬁnd what you’re looking for just from the the Instant Information boxes.

Facts

Harvix provides numerous facts about your query. Facts are pulled directly from web sites that Harvix deems are reliable and accurate.

Categorization

Results on Harvix are seperated into digestable catagories which allows you to find exactly what you are looking for faster. For example "Steve Jobs" = Instant Information, Facts, Twitter, Images, Social, Apple, Investing, Money, Tech, and Latest.

Spam-Free Web Results

Harvix only includes sites whose primary purpose is information, not monetization in our web results. Sites created solely to sell you products or steal your money are not included in our web results.

Notes

With Harvix’s unique notes feature you can take notes directly from your search page. Your notes are saved in the cloud, and are accessible from any device.
